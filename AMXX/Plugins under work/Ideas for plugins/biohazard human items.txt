The Items : 

1-Unlimited Clip ( 50 AP )
2-Noclip ( 75 AP )
3-100 Armor ( 15 AP )
4-Multijump ( 25 AP )  
5-Grenades Menu ( 30 AP )

___________________

Cvars 

amx_cvar bio_hmitems "1" / 1 = on , 2 = off
amx_cvar bio_noclip "5" / How Many Seconds Till Its Over
amx_cvar bio_unlimitedclip "1" / How Many Rounds Till Its over
amx_cvar bio_armor "1" / 1 = 1 round then its back to 0 , 2 = round finishs and still 100 armor
amx_cvar bio_jetpack "10" / How Many Seconds Till Its Over
amx_cvar bio_multijumphm "4" / How Many Jumps Can He Jump

____________________

Commands :

/items / Opens up the items store
/myap / Shows how much ammo packs does he have

____________________

Admin Commands :

/giveap / gives a player ammo packs
/takeap / Take from a player ammo packs
