Discreption :

You Know How To Play Freeze tag Cops must catch the terrorist and the terrorist must run from the cops , but when the cops caught u and the other player is busy to run what will u do to untag ur self , well this plugin may help u :)

________________

How To Do It : 

Well When the Cops Catch u You Just Must Find a Way To Untag ur self By Typing /untag
Well This Will Cost You Some Time , Like Will Cost u 10 Minutes Of Your Original Time

________________

Cvars : 

amx_cvar ft_untag "1" / 1 = on , 2  = off
amx_cvar ft_untagcost "10" / This Will Cost The Player 10 Minutes of his original time :)

________________

commands :

/untag

_________________

Admin Commands :

/freeuntag / This Wont Cost The Admins Any Minutes

Lang : 

Player %name Has Seccussfully Untagged Him Self And Payed 10 Minutes of his time
